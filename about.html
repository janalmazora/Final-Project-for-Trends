What is Singer's Hub?

       Singer's Hub reinvents the karaoke. It is a whole new experience in sing-a-long style of enjoyment. It is free and it is available everywhere you go. It is an karaoke that is flexible for change.

Why we developed this?

       We developed Singer's Hub due to the progressive innovation that is happening nowadays. Smart TVs, Smart Phones, Tablets, and Desktop PCs can now access the internet within a click of a button. That's why we thought of developing a karaoke that people will be able to access whatever devices they are using.
